## Preface
<h3>-- The Human Body is a Holographic Open System</h3>

> The dilemma of modern mainstream medicine lies in the overuse of "reductionism" — disassembling life into organs, organs into cells, and cells into molecules. However, life is not a pile of parts, but the emergence of a system.
The <b>"Life Systems Theory"</b> proposed by HHPF views the human body as a multi-dimensional, open complex giant system. We believe that health is the dynamic balance within the system and between the system and the environment. To understand this balance, we must master the three cornerstone elements that constitute life: <b>Matter, Energy, and Information</b>.



## I. The Three Cornerstones of Life Systems: Matter, Energy, Information
We decompose the constituent elements of life systems using MECE (Mutually Exclusive, Collectively Exhaustive). Any disease occurrence and cure cannot escape the interaction of these three dimensions.

### 1. Matter Layer: The Hardware Foundation of Life
*   <b>Definition:</b> The physical existence that constitutes the body. Including genes, proteins, cells, tissues, organs, bones, blood, and ingested nutrients.
*   <b>Corresponding Medicine:</b> Most proficient in <b>Western Medicine</b> and <b>nutrition</b>.
*   <b>HHPF View:</b> Matter is the carrier of life. Damage at the matter layer (such as fractures, solid tumors, vascular plaques) is usually the <b>final result</b> of system imbalance, not the initial cause.

### 2. Energy Layer: The Driving Force of Life
*   <b>Definition:</b> The power that drives matter to operate. Including bioelectricity, heat energy, metabolic energy (ATP), as well as the "Qi" in Traditional Chinese Medicine and the energy flow conducted through meridians.
*   <b>Corresponding Medicine:</b> <b>Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)</b>, <b>natural therapy</b> (light therapy, heat therapy, exercise).
*   <b>HHPF View:</b> Energy is the fuel of life. Many sub-healthy states that "cannot be diagnosed" are essentially "energy depletion" or "energy stagnation."

### 3. Information Layer: The Command System of Life
*   <b>Definition:</b> The instruction set that controls matter and energy.
    *   *Microscopic:* Genetic coding of DNA/RNA, hormone signals, neurotransmitters.
    *   *Macroscopic:* <b>Consciousness</b>, emotions, beliefs, subconscious, social psychological pressure.
*   <b>Corresponding Medicine:</b> <b>Psychotherapy</b>, <b>mind-body medicine</b>, <b>SMD Enlightenment Training</b>.
*   <b>HHPF View:</b> Information determines structure. Negative information (such as long-term fear, anxiety) can rewrite biochemical instructions, leading to energy disorder and material lesions.

Based on the three cornerstones, we redefine the core concepts of medical care:

*   <b>Essence of Health:</b> Complete structure at the matter layer, smooth flow at the energy layer, and clear instructions at the information layer. All three are in a <b>low-entropy (ordered) state</b>.
*   <b>Evolution of Disease:</b> Often follows the path of <b>Information Imbalance → Energy Stagnation → Material Lesions</b>.
