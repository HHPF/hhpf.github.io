# First International Health Systems Forum Successfully Held in Beijing

<b>Release Date</b>: 2025-12-15

## Forum Overview

The first International Health Systems Forum, hosted by the Human Health Plan Foundation, was successfully held in Beijing. More than 300 experts and scholars from over 20 countries around the world participated in this forum to discuss health system innovation and development.

## Forum Theme

With the theme of "Health System Innovation and Sustainable Development", this forum conducted in-depth discussions on topics such as health system theory, health data sharing, the application of artificial intelligence in health, and global health governance.

## Distinguished Guests

Distinguished guests attending the forum included:
- Director-General of the World Health Organization
- Minister of the National Health Commission of China
- Director of the U.S. National Institutes of Health
- President of the Royal College of Physicians of the UK
- Renowned experts and scholars in the global health field

## Forum Achievements

The forum achieved fruitful results:
1. Released the "Declaration on International Health System Innovation"
2. Established the "Global Health System Innovation Alliance"
3. Launched 10 international health cooperation projects
4. Signed 5 international health cooperation agreements

## Media Coverage

The forum received widespread attention from global media, including Xinhua News Agency, People's Daily, BBC, CNN, and other mainstream media at home and abroad, which all reported on the forum.

## Follow-up Activities

The Human Health Plan Foundation plans to hold an International Health Systems Forum annually, aiming to build it into an important exchange platform in the global health field.
