# Volunteer Recruitment

## [Preface: Don't Be a Bystander to History, Be an Agent of Change]
The upgrading of human health civilization requires a spark, and even more requires a prairie fire.
HHPF is looking for <b>"Health Awakened Individuals"</b> worldwide. No matter where you are, no matter what your occupation is, as long as you agree with the health view of "First Principles" and have an altruistic heart of "ultimate kindness", you are the <b>HHPF Global Health Ambassador</b> we are looking for.
Here, every hour you put in will be transformed into a turning point in someone's life; every piece of wisdom you contribute will become the cornerstone of the SMD system benefiting humanity.



## I. Recruitment Targets: Three Core Groups (MECE Classification)
We need strength from different dimensions. According to skill attributes and contribution methods, we divide volunteer positions into three mutually exclusive and exhaustive groups:

### 1. Professional Intelligence Group
<b>——"Accelerate Change with Skills"</b>
We need talents with specific professional skills to participate in core project construction in a distributed collaborative manner.
*   <b>Medical/Scientific Volunteers:</b>
    *   *Needs:* Doctors, nutritionists, traditional Chinese medicine practitioners, psychological counselors, biologists.
    *   *Tasks:* Participate in the review and revision of SOPs, assist in organizing the RAG medical case library, and participate in the efficacy verification of standardized engineering.
*   <b>Technical/Geek Volunteers:</b>
    *   *Needs:* AI engineers, data scientists, full-stack developers.
    *   *Tasks:* Participate in open-source code contributions, data cleaning, and algorithm optimization for the SMD system.
*   <b>Content/Communication Volunteers:</b>
    *   *Needs:* Translators (multilingual), medical illustrators, video creators, popular science writers.
    *   *Tasks:* Transform profound Life Systems Theory into easy-to-understand multilingual content, eliminating knowledge barriers.

### 2. Compassionate Service Group
<b>——"Soothe Life with Love"</b>
We need partners who are willing to invest time and emotion to directly serve beneficiaries offline or online.
*   <b>SMD Online Companions:</b>
    *   *Tasks:* In the SMD community, provide listening and psychological support to help seekers with anxiety and depression (requires HHPF training).
*   <b>Community Micro-Station Managers:</b>
    *   *Tasks:* Assist in operating HHPF community health micro-stations, organize offline book clubs, meditation sessions, or elderly care activities.
*   <b>Emergency Assistance Liaisons:</b>
    *   *Tasks:* Assist in verifying the authenticity of "public welfare medical care" applicants to ensure that donations are used properly.

### 3. Data Pioneer Group
<b>——"Verify Truth with Flesh"</b>
We need pioneers who dare to try new paradigms.
*   <b>Therapy Experience Officers (Patient Experts):</b>
    *   *Needs:* Patients with specific chronic diseases who are willing to try non-drug natural therapies.
    *   *Tasks:* Conduct self-intervention strictly according to HHPF's SOP, and truthfully record and upload physical and mental data to become a valuable sample for A/B testing (practitioners of "self as science").



## II. Reciprocal Mechanism: Giving is Receiving
At HHPF, volunteer service is not a one-way payment, but a high-dimensional <b>energy exchange</b>. We provide volunteers with extremely rich non-monetary rewards:

1.  <b>Mental Upgrading Privilege:</b>
    *   All official volunteers who pass the assessment will <b>free of charge</b> receive the valuable "SMD Enlightenment Training Camp" beginner and intermediate courses. We first help you "become a new human", and then you help others.
2.  <b>SMD Pro Usage Rights:</b>
    *   Free access to advanced membership rights of the SMD artificial intelligence system (including more in-depth health analysis reports, priority consultation channels).
3.  <b>Professional Certification Channel:</b>
    *   Outstanding volunteers can be prioritized for HHPF "Health Coach" or "Enlightenment Instructor" professional training scholarships, or even directly converted to <b>full-time employees</b>.
4.  <b>Honor and Identity:</b>
    *   Blockchain-based <b>"HHPF Good Deeds Certificate"</b> (tamper-proof, permanently recording your contributions).
    *   Invited to participate in HHPF's global annual summit, face-to-face with top experts.



## III. Joining Process: Rigorous SOP
To ensure the purity and professionalism of the team, we have a strict access mechanism:

1.  <b>Submit Application (Application):</b>
    *   Fill in the "HHPF Volunteer Application Form" online, detailing skills and values cognition.
2.  <b>Values Screening (Screening):</b>
    *   Through AI initial screening + manual review, ensure that your original intention is "truth-seeking and ultimate kindness", not just for commercial interests.
3.  <b>General Knowledge Training (Training):</b>
    *   Complete the online training on "HHPF Vision and Mission" and basic health knowledge, and pass the exam.
4.  <b>Probation and Confirmation (Probation):</b>
    *   Enter a 1-month probation period and complete at least 3 volunteer tasks. Those who pass will be officially awarded the "HHPF Global Volunteer" badge.
